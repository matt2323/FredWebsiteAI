Our Story

A Neighborhood Tradition

For nearly two decades, Wine & Spirits at 130 Main has been more than just a liquor store — we're a fixture of the Boonville community. Whether you're hunting for the perfect bottle for a special occasion or stopping in for your weekend staple, our friendly, knowledgeable staff is always here to help.

We take pride in curating a thoughtful selection that celebrates the outstanding wines of New York State alongside bottles from the world's finest wine regions — all with the personal touch you can only find at a true neighborhood shop. We accept cash, Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover.

What We Carry

Our Selection

Wine

Explore a curated collection spanning the globe — from bold Napa Cabernets and crisp Italian whites to approachable everyday bottles that won't break the bank. Something for every palate and every occasion.

Spirits

From premium single-malt scotches and craft bourbons to tequila, gin, rum, and vodka — we stock the classics and love helping you discover something new. Ask us for a recommendation anytime.

New York Made

Local Favorites

Proud to spotlight the best of New York State — Finger Lakes Rieslings, Hudson Valley reds, and craft spirits made right here in the Empire State. Support local, drink exceptionally well.
